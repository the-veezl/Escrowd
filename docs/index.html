// how it works

Three steps.
Zero trust needed.

escrowd acts as a cryptographic middleman. Funds are locked on the Stellar blockchain until delivery is confirmed.

01 / LOCK 🔒

Alice locks the deal

Alice types !escrow lock bob 10. A unique Stellar wallet is created. The secret is sent to Alice's DMs only.

02 / DELIVER 📦

Bob delivers the goods

Bob sees the deal is locked on-chain. He delivers the digital goods, knowing funds are secured cryptographically.

03 / CLAIM ✅

Alice reveals, Bob claims

Alice shares the secret. Bob types !escrow claim. Funds release automatically on Stellar. Done.

04 / DISPUTE ⚖️

Something went wrong?

Raise a dispute. Deal freezes instantly. Submit evidence. Free 24h auto-resolution or KES 60 for 15-minute priority review.

// features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for real traders, not enterprise clients.

⛓️

Stellar Blockchain Custody

Every deal gets its own Stellar wallet. Funds move on-chain automatically when deals are claimed. Full transaction history on stellar.expert.

🔐

SHA-256 Hash Locks

Secrets are never stored — only their SHA-256 fingerprint. One wrong character fails the claim. Cryptographically impossible to fake.

⚖️

Dispute Resolution

Raise a dispute and the deal freezes instantly. Submit evidence links. Free auto-resolution in 24h or KES 60 for priority 15-minute review.

📋

Immutable Audit Trail

Every action is recorded permanently with timestamp and actor. Nobody can deny what happened. Run !escrow history anytime.

🛡️

Brute Force Protection

Exponential backoff on failed claim attempts: 1s, 2s, 4s, 8s, 16s, 32s — then locked for 1 hour. Automated scripts cannot brute-force secrets.

🔄

Auto-Expiry

Deals that aren't claimed automatically refund after 48 hours. No funds get stuck forever. Disputed deals also auto-resolve on expiry.
